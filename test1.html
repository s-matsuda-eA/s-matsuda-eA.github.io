From the popular series, a limited design drawstring purse set is now available!

From the STATION WORKERS series with motifs of people working in each city, a drawstring bag set exclusively available at the online store is now available!

This set is perfect for yourself or as a gift, with an assortment of regular-sized dry fries packed into a drawstring bag with an illustration of "IT GUY" depicting a man working at an IT company.

AND THE FRIET carefully processes the ingredients of French fries ordered from all over the world, and takes time to bring out the crispy texture in a special fryer, making the best use of the quality and deliciousness of the ingredients.

The package was designed by art director Naomi Hirabayashi. The illustrations are drawn by European illustrator Anje Jager.

Flavor lineup


PREMIUM SALT - Premium SaltSprinkled with French Lorraine rock salt, which has a mellow taste and contains natural minerals. Enjoy the taste of the royal road.
CHEDDAR CHEESE - Cheddar cheeseEnjoy the aroma and rich flavor of aged cheddar cheese sprinkled generously. It has a rich taste that can be enjoyed as a snack with wine.
DOUBLE-TRUFFLE SALT - Double truffle saltWe doubled the amount of black truffle salt, which is the most popular of all time, to make it even more delicious. Black truffles from Italy add a gorgeous fragrance.

It is not possible to specify a delivery time between 12:00 and 14:00.

Please note that even if the item is in stock, shipping arrangements can only be made during business hours on weekdays, and we cannot accept deliveries shorter than the dates listed below.

Tohoku, Kanto, Chubu, Kinki: 3 days of middle school / Hokkaido, Chugoku, Shikoku, Kyushu: 4 days of middle school / Okinawa: 5 or more days of middle school
